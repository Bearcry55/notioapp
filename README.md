## Requirements

- Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher
- Notification permissions (will be requested on first use)

## How to Use

1. Open the app
2. Enter your custom notification message
3. Set the time for your reminder
4. Save the notification
5. The app will remind you at the specified time
6. If you miss it, you'll get reminded again every minute

## Permissions

This app requires the following permissions:
- **Notifications**: To display your custom reminders
- **Alarm & Reminders**: To schedule notifications at specific times
